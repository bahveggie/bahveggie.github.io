Al Dente Lentil Salad

Ingredients

  • Kale
  • Almonds
  • Scallions
  • Garlic cloves
  • Lemon
  • Black beluga or French green lentils
  • Cumin seeds
  • Crushed red pepper flakes

Instructions

  • Cook lentils al dente.
  • Shred kale. Slice almonds and scallions.
  • Mince garlic cloves.
  • Mix together.
