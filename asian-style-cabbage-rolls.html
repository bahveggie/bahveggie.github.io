Asian-Style Cabbage Rolls

Ingredients

  • Napa cabbage
  • Carrots
  • Ginger root
  • Coconut aminos (optional)

Instructions

  • Clean and place whole cabbage in steaming water, turning and removing leaves as they become pliable.
  • Shred carrots and red cabbage. Chop onions. Remove skin and mince ginger root. Saute carrots, red cabbage, and onions with minced ginger. Douse with cocnut aminos.
  • Scoop the saute mixture into cabbage leaves, near the base. Roll leaves and fold in edges around the mixture.
