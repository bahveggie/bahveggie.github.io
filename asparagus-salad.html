Asparagus Salad

Ingredients

  • Asparagus, cooked and sliced on bias
  • Avocado, cubed
  • Black eyed peas, cooked
  • Almonds, halved
  • Cilantro or purslane or pea shoots
  • Lemon juice/zest
  • Caraway seeds or fennel seeds

Instructions

  • Mix all ingredients together.
