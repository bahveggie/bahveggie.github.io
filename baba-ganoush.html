Baba Ganoush

Ingredients

  • Eggplant
  • Sesame seeds
  • Garlic powder
  • Lemon juice
  • Black pepper

Instructions

  • Cut eggplant lengthwise. Roast in oven. Remove skin.
  • Add all ingredients; blend in Vitamix until desired consistency.
