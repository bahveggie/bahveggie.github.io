Broccoli Quinoa Salad

Ingredients

  • Chia seeds
  • Green beans or yellow beans, al dente, chopped
  • Sugar snap peas
  • Fresh herbs parsley, chives, basil, tarragon, dill
  • Mustard seeds
  • Black pepper
  • Broccoli
  • Pea shoots
  • Quinoa

Instructions

  • Cook quinoa.
  • Cut broccoli into florets.
  • Mix all ingredients.
