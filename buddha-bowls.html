Buddha Bowls

Ingredients

  • Quinoa
  • Chickpeas
  • Literally any cooked or raw vegetables

Instructions

  • Cook and drain chickpeas.
  • Cook quinoa.
  • Add quinoa to the bottom of the bowl and arrange chickpeas and vegetables on top.
