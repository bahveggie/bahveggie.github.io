Cauliflower White Bean Mash

Ingredients

  • Cauliflower
  • White beans
  • Dried parsley
  • Garlic powder

Instructions

  • Cook white beans and reserve liquid.
  • Cook cauliflower.
  • Place cauliflower in Vitamix blender. Then add cooked white beans. Pour enough of the reserved bean cooking water in the Vitamix.
  • Blend until smooth.
  • Add dried parsley and garlic powder and blend until constituted.
