Collard Green Rolls

Ingredients

  • Collard greens
  • Carrots
  • Green peas
  • Millet
  • Basil leaves
  • Broccoli
  • Green onions
  • Kale
  • Black-eyed peas
  • Walnuts or sunflower seeds
  • Lemon juice

Instructions

  • Steam collard greens.
  • Arrange chosen toppings on a collard green leaf and roll it up, folding in the sides.
