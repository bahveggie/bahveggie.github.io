White Beans in Red Bell Pepper Sauce

Ingredients

  • Cucumbers
  • White beans
  • Red onion
  • Fresh dill
  • Lemon juice

Instructions

  • Quarter cucumbers lengthwise and then slice.
  • Chop onion and fresh dill.
  • Combine all ingredients and serve cold.
