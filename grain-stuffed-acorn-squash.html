Grain Stuffed Acorn Squash

Ingredients

  • Acorn squash
  • Millet
  • Buckwheat
  • Kale
  • Cranberries
  • Walnuts

Instructions

  • Cook millet, buckwheat, and cranberries all in one sauce pan.
  • Shred and steam kale in the microwave.
  • Combine kale and walnuts into sauce pan.
  • Scoop into cooked acorn halves and return to oven for 15 minutes.
