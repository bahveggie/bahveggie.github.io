Green Salad

Ingredients

  • Asparagus
  • Broccoli
  • Avocado
  • Quinoa
  • Scallions
  • Garlic cloves
  • Mixed baby greens
  • Frozen peas
  • Hemp seeds
  • Fresh basil
  • Lemon juice
  • Black pepper

Instructions

  • Chop and cook asparagus.
  • Cook quinoa.
  • Cut up scallions and avocado.
  • Mix everything together.
