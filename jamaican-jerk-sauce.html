Jamaican Jerk Sauce

Ingredients

  • 4-6 Scotch Bonnet peppers
  • 1 small red onion
  • 4-6 garlic cloves
  • 4 stalks scallions
  • 1/4 cup vinegar
  • 1/2 lime juiced
  • 1 tbsp fresh ginger
  • 1 tsp nutmeg
  • 1 tsp allspice
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 tsp thyme
  • 1 tbsp black pepper

Instructions

  • Food processor until desired consistency.

Simple Version

  • Just scotch bonnet peppers, escallions, spices
