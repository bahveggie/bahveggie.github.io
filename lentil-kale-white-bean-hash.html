Lentil, Kale, White Bean Hash

Ingredients

  • Beluga lentils
  • White beans
  • Shredded kale
  • Walnuts

Instructions

  • Cook white beans. Cook beluga lentils.
  • Saute shredded kale, remove from pan, saute crumbled walnuts.
  • Combine all ingredients.
