Millet and Spinach (pasta alternative)

Ingredients

  • Millet
  • Spinach
  • Chickpeas
  • Avocado

Instructions

  • Cook millet with the fresh spinach in the top of the pan so it wilts.
  • Once millet is cooked, mix in spinach and cooked chickpeas, and heat through.
  • Serve each bowl with a half of an avocado cut in slices.
