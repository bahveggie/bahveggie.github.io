Polish-Style Cabbage Rolls

Ingredients, All herbs are dried

  • Cabbage
  • Brown or green lentils
  • Quinoa
  • San Marzano tomato sauce (optional)
  • Oregano
  • Thyme

Instructions

  • Cook lentils and drain.
  • Cook quinoa.
  • Mix lentils and quinoa with tomato sauce, oregano, and thyme.
  • Clean and place whole cabbage in steaming water, turning and removing leaves as they become pliable.
  • Scoop the saute mixture into cabbage leaves, near the base. Roll leaves and fold in edges around the mixture.
