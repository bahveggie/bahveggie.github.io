Roasted Chickpeas and Brussels Sprouts

Ingredients

  • Cooked chickpeas
  • Fresh Brussels sprouts
  • Black pepper
  • Sunflower seeds

Instructions

  • Halve brussels sprouts. Arrange brussels and chickpeas onto a baking tray and bake until roasted.
  • Mix in sunflower seeds and season with black pepper to taste (have it available on the table).
