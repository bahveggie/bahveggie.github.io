Rutabaga Stuffed Butternut Squash

Ingredients

  • Rutabaga
  • Cinnamon
  • Walnuts
  • Sunflower seeds

Instructions

  • Halve and de-seed butternut squash. Roast cut-side down in a water pan.
  • Crumble walnuts.
  • Toast walnuts and sunflower seeds.
  • Peel and cut up rutabaga. Boil until fork goes through. Drain.
  • Sprinkle rutabaga with cinnamon.
  • Add walnuts and sunflower seeds to the pot and stir.
  • Add mixture to the wells in roasted butternut squash halves. Sprinkle with cinnamon.
