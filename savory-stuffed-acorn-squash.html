Savory Stuffed Acorn Squash

Ingredients

  • Acorn squash
  • Black beans
  • Broccoli
  • Onions

Instructions

  • Cook black beans.
  • Water saute onion and broccoli. Mix in black beans once cooked and onions are translucent.
  • Scoop into roasted acorn squash halves.
