Shepherd's Pie

Ingredients, All herbs are dried

  • Cauliflower White Bean Mash
  • Brown or green lentils
  • Quinoa
  • Mushrooms
  • Onion
  • Carrot
  • Celery
  • San Marzano tomato sauce (optional)
  • Rosemary
  • Garlic
  • Black pepper
  • Flax meal
  • Chives or parsley

Instructions

  • Cook and drain lentils.
  • Cook quinoa.
  • Chop all vegetables.
  • In a large frying pan, add onion, mushrooms, celery, and carrots and cook on medium-high heat. Add optional San Marzano tomato sauce. Season with rosemary, garlic, and black pepper. Add flax meal for health and to thicken the sauce.
  • Combine frying pan mixture with lentils and quinoa.
  • Place mixture in the bottom of a pie pan. Top with Cauiflower White Bean mash and chives or parsley.
