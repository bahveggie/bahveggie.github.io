Simple Meatballs over Salad and Fresh Herbs

Ingredients

  • Eggplant and millet flour meatballs
  • Iceberg lettuce
  • Fresh cilantro sprigs
  • Fresh mint
  • Tart green apples

Instructions

  • Cut up apples.
  • Arrange iceberg lettuce, apples, cilantro, and mint leaves in sections in the bowl like a buddha bowl.
  • Add warm meatballs on top.
