Slow Cooker Apples

Ingredients

  • Apples
  • Cinnamon
  • Walnuts
  • Cranberries or raisins

Instructions

  • Core and slice apples.
  • Layer in a slow cooker with cinnamon, walnuts, and cranberries or raisins between layers.
  • Slow cooker on high for about an hour until the apples are cooked through.
