Slow Cooker Applesauce

Ingredients

  • Apples
  • Cranberries
  • Beets
  • Lemon juice

Instructions

  • Peel and chop beets. Add to slow cooker.
  • Add fresh cranberries to the slow cooker.
  • Core and chop apples. Add to slow cooker.
  • Sprinkle with lemon juice.
  • Slow cook on high until beets are soft enough.
  • Blend with immersion blender.
