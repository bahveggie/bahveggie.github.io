Spicy Salad

Ingredients

  • Butter lettuce
  • Radishes
  • Cucumbers
  • Sugar snap peas
  • Chives
  • Roast nuts
  • Hot pepper sauce

Instructions

  • On a bed of butter lettuce, add radishes, sliced cucumbers, sugar snap peas, chopped chives, roast nuts.
  • Dress in hot pepper sauce.
