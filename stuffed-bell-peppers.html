Stuffed Bell Peppers

Ingredients, All herbs are dried

  • Black beans
  • Quinoa
  • Basil
  • Oregano
  • Bell peppers

Instructions

  • Cook and drain black beans.
  • Cook quinoa with basil and oregano.
  • Mix together quinoa and black beans.
  • Halve bell peppers, remove green and seeds. Arrange on a baking tray, cut side up.
  • Scoop mixture into each bell pepper half. Bake until it smells like the bell peppers are roasted.
