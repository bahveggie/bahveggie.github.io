White Beans in Red Bell Pepper Sauce

Ingredients

  • Cooked white beans
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 onion
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1/4 tsp fresh thyme (halve for dried)
  • 1/4 tsp fresh rosemary (halve for dried)
  • 1/3 c pureeed roasted red bell peppers
  • 1 c water (or other stock e.g. reserved from cooking beans)
  • Parsley sunflower pesto for garnish: 1 c fresh parsley, 1 garlic, Â¼ c sunflower seeds, 2 tbsp water

Instructions

  • Toast sunflower seeds in hot pan.
  • Water saute onions and garlic, thyme, and rosemary.
  • Add water (or broth), stir in pureed bell pepper, and add bay leaf.
  • Simmer, then add cooked beans in to warm.
  • Garnish a swirl of pesto on top of the beans in the pan.
